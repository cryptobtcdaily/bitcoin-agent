About CryptoDaily

Your trusted, independent source for daily Bitcoin and cryptocurrency news, market data, and education.

Our Mission

CryptoDaily was built with one goal in mind — to make cryptocurrency news and data accessible to everyone. Whether you are a complete beginner hearing about Bitcoin for the first time, or an experienced trader tracking daily market movements, CryptoDaily gives you everything you need in one place.

We believe that access to clear, unbiased, and timely financial information should not be locked behind paywalls or buried under technical jargon. Our content is always free, always updated, and written in plain language that anyone can understand.

Every day we bring you the latest Bitcoin headlines, full market data across the top 50 cryptocurrencies, trending coin analysis, whale transaction monitoring, and the Fear & Greed index — all in one clean, easy-to-navigate website.

Our Data Sources

We believe in full transparency about where our data comes from. CryptoDaily uses only reputable, publicly available data sources:

CoinGecko API

Price data, market caps, trading volumes, trending coins, and all cryptocurrency market statistics. One of the world's most trusted crypto data providers.

Alternative.me

The Fear & Greed Index and its 7-day historical data. This index analyses volatility, market momentum, social media, and surveys to produce a daily sentiment score.

Blockchain.info

Live Bitcoin blockchain data including unconfirmed transactions, whale movements, and network statistics directly from the Bitcoin network.

RSS News Feeds

News sourced daily from CoinTelegraph, Bitcoin Magazine, and CoinDesk — three of the most established and respected publications in the cryptocurrency space.
